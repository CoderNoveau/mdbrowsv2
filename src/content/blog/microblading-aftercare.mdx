Taking care of your brows after microblading is crucial for the best results. Here's what you should do:

1. Avoid getting your brows wet for 7 days.
2. Don't pick or scratch the area.
3. Apply healing balm as recommended.
4. Avoid heavy makeup on the brow area.

![Microblading Aftercare](/images/service-microblading.webp)

Follow these steps and your brows will heal beautifully!
